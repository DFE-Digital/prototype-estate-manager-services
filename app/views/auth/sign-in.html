Department for Education Sign-in

Department for Education (DfE) Sign-in is how schools and other education organisations access DfE online services.

To access DfE online services, you'll either need:

  • an existing DfE Sign-in account
  • to create a DfE Sign-in account if you haven't used DfE Sign-in before
