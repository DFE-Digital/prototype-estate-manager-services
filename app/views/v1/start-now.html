Manage your education estate

If you are responsible for overseeing or maintaining education estates, sign in to this service to:

  • plan maintenance and improvements

  • understand the condition of your estate

  • report on spending

  • explore funding options

  • get guidance on planning and construction

  • find out how to get emergency support

Accessing the service

You will need to:

  • access through an existing DfE Sign-in account

  • create a DfE Sign-in account
