Every Responsible Body has legal duties to ensure that the buildings and grounds are:

  • Safe
  • well-maintained
  • suitable for students, staff, and visitors

The following responsibilities come from laws and regulations that must be followed, and best practice actions that will help you manage your estates effectively.

CDM regulations compliance

Make sure construction projects comply with the Construction (Design and Management) Regulations 2015.

Planning permission

Secure required planning permissions and ensure compliance with listed building regulations where applicable.

Contractor due diligence

Conduct due diligence when engaging contractors for estate work.

Fire and safety standards

Make sure new construction meets fire safety regulations and accessibility requirements.

Flood and climate resilience

Use environment agency data to assess flood risks and implement climate adaptation strategies.
