Every Responsible Body has legal duties to ensure that the buildings and grounds are:

  • Safe
  • well-maintained
  • suitable for students, staff, and visitors

The following responsibilities come from laws and regulations that must be followed, and best practice actions that will help you manage your estates effectively.

Estate strategy

Develop a long-term (3-5 years) estate strategy linked to the school's educational vision.

Asset management plan

Align the asset management plan with estate priorities, condition data, and maintenance requirements.

Risk assessments

Maintain a risk assessment register to identify and mitigate potential estate risks.

Budgeting and planning

Make sure accurate budget forecasts based on past data and risk considerations.

Planned preventative maintenance

Develop a balance between planned and reactive maintenance.

Sustainability planning

Incorporate sustainability measures, including energy efficiency and climate resilience.
