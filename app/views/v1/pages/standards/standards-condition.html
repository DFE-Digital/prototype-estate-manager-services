Every Responsible Body has legal duties to ensure that the buildings and grounds are:

  • Safe
  • well-maintained
  • suitable for students, staff, and visitors

The following responsibilities come from laws and regulations that must be followed, and best practice actions that will help you manage your estates effectively.

Condition surveys

Regular condition surveys to understand the state of buildings and infrastructure.

Asset register

Maintain an up-to-date asset register, including:

  • tenure
  • building area
  • statutory compliance records

Statutory inspections

Ensure all plant and equipment requiring statutory inspections are documented and inspected.

Asbestos management

Conduct asbestos surveys and maintain an asbestos management plan.

Fire safety

Have a comprehensive fire risk assessment and regularly test fire detection systems.

Health and safety compliance

Implement a health and safety policy covering all estate-related risks.

Structural stability

Conduct periodic inspections for structural integrity and report any visible changes

Legal interests and land ownership

Ensure clarity on the terms of occupation and permissions required for any estate-related work.
