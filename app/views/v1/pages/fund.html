Legal responsibilities around seeking funding options to implement your improvements

Every Responsible Body has legal duties to ensure that the buildings and grounds are safe, well-maintained, and suitable for students, staff, and visitors. The following responsibilities come from laws and regulations that must be followed, and best practice actions that will help you manage your estates effectively.

  • Budget Prioritisation: Ensure that funding allocation considers estate risk assessments.
  • Procurement Compliance: Follow public sector procurement guidelines to ensure regularity, propriety, and value for money.
  • Capital and Revenue Budgets: Consider the full cost of occupying the estate and the long-term financial implications of investments.
  • Grant Applications: Align funding applications with legal and strategic estate management requirements.
  • Governance Oversight: Ensure governing boards oversee estate finances and have the necessary skills to support estate management decisions.
