Commission and oversee construction and maintenance work

Informed and careful project planning can make sure you:

  • have the right people to help you achieve your desired outcomes
  • make the best use of your resources
  • understand your school or organisation's responsibilities before the project starts, during the project, and after it has been completed

Your legal responsibilities

You have legal duties to make sure that the buildings and grounds are:

  • safe
  • well-maintained
  • suitable for students, staff, and visitors

Understand your legal responsibilities when commissioning and overseeing construction and maintenance work.

Guidance on construction work

The guidance for commissioning and overseeing contruction and maintenance work will help you improve building and workplace safety.

  • The Construction Playbook
  • Employer's requirements part B: generic design brief
  • School buildings: construction framework 2021
  • School and Further Education College Design and Construction Guidance

For commissioning work, you can use the list of DfE approved contractors.
